# Overview

An internal application where WSO2 employees request time off and managers approve or reject those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their balance
- Manager — reviews and responds to leave requests from their team

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request by choosing dates and a leave type.
- Managers see a list of pending requests from people on their team.
- A manager approves or rejects each request with an optional comment.
- Employees view the status of their submitted requests.
- Everyone can check how many days they have available for each leave type.
